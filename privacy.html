Privacy Policy

Last Updated:

VX Link Helper ("the Extension") was built with user privacy as its core principle. This policy explains how your data is handled when you use the Extension.

1. Zero Data Collection

We do not collect, store, transmit, or sell any of your personal data.

The Extension operates entirely locally within your browser. There are no background analytics, no telemetry, no tracking pixels, and no communication with external servers managed by the developer.

2. How the Extension Works

All link processing—stripping tracking parameters and rewriting URLs (e.g., converting x.com to vxtwitter.com)—happens locally on your device using JavaScript. Your browsing history and the links you copy are never sent to us.

3. Required Permissions and Why We Need Them

To function correctly, the Extension requests the following browser permissions. Here is exactly what they are used for:

  • contextMenus: Allows us to add the "复制 VX 链接" (Copy VX Link) option when you right-click on a web page.
  • clipboardWrite: Required to automatically copy the cleaned, tracking-free URL to your clipboard.
  • <all_urls> / Host Permissions: Required to detect when you are visiting supported websites (like Twitter, Bilibili, Reddit, and Pixiv) so the Extension can inject the custom sharing buttons next to the native UI.

4. Third-Party Services

The Extension does not integrate with any third-party APIs or analytics services. It functions as a standalone, offline-capable tool within your browser environment.

5. Changes to This Policy

Because the Extension does not collect data, this policy is unlikely to change. However, if major architectural changes are made to the Extension, we will update this page accordingly.

6. Contact & Source Code

VX Link Helper is 100% open-source. You can verify our privacy claims by reading the entire codebase yourself.

If you have questions or concerns about this policy, please open an issue on our GitHub Repository.

VX Link Helper is 100% open-source, local-only, and tracking-free.
